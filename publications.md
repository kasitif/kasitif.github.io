## Research Impact

My research has been presented at leading international conferences including:
- **IGARSS** (International Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium)
- **EUSAR** (European Conference on Synthetic Aperture Radar)
- **InGARSS** (India Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium)

### Key Research Themes

**Flood Mapping with SAR**: Developing innovative polarimetric decomposition techniques to improve flood detection accuracy in complex environments.

**Environmental Monitoring**: Using satellite radar to track invasive species and support ecosystem management in East Africa.

**Operational Systems**: Translating research into practical tools for disaster risk reduction and water resources management.

**Community Engagement**: Building capacity and empowering local communities through accessible geospatial technologies.

## Ongoing Research

### PhD Thesis (Expected 2026)
**Title**: Detecting and monitoring floods using satellite radar and drone data

This research develops novel methodologies for flood extent mapping using polarimetric SAR data from multiple sensors (Sentinel-1, ALOS-2, UAVSAR). The work includes field validation with drone technology and integration with machine learning for automated flood detection.
