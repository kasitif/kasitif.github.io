## Journal Articles

**Isundwa, F. K.**, Bhatt, C. M., Bhangale, U., Mukherjee, S., et al. (2024). *Polarimetric SAR-Based Monitoring of Water Hyacinth Infestation in Lake Victoria Using ALOS-2 Quad-Pol Data*. IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing (JSTARS).  
First application of quad-polarimetric SAR decomposition for invasive aquatic vegetation monitoring in East Africa; demonstrated seasonal infestation dynamics across the lake.

**Isundwa, F. K.**, et al. (Under Review). *Flood Extent Mapping in Vegetated Wetlands Using Polarimetric SAR Decomposition Techniques*.  
Submitted to *IEEE Transactions on Geoscience and Remote Sensing*. Proposes a novel decomposition-based water detection framework validated against drone surveys in Scottish floodplains.

## Conference Proceedings

**Isundwa, F. K.**, et al. (2024). *Polarimetric Decomposition Approaches for Flood Detection Under Vegetation: A Comparative Analysis*. In *Proceedings of IGARSS 2024* (IEEE International Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium).  
Compared Freeman-Durden, Yamaguchi, and H/A/Alpha decompositions for flood signal separation in vegetated terrain.

**Isundwa, F. K.**, et al. (2024). *Ground-Based Polarimetric Radar for Controlled Flood Simulation Experiments*. In *Proceedings of EUSAR 2024* (European Conference on Synthetic Aperture Radar).  
Presented experimental design and early results from controlled floodplain inundation trials using ground-based full-polarimetric radar.

**Isundwa, F. K.**, et al. (2023). *Detecting Water Hyacinth in Lake Victoria Using ALOS-2 Polarimetric SAR*. In *Proceedings of InGARSS 2023* (Indian Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium).  
Initial findings from polarimetric classification of aquatic vegetation in freshwater lake environments.

## Technical Reports

Scottish Environment Protection Agency (SEPA). (2025). *Social Media and Earth Observation Integration for Disaster Response: Methodology and Pilot Results.*  
Contributed deep learning pipeline design, geotagged data processing workflows, and integration with SEPA's operational flood forecasting system.

SERVIR Eastern & Southern Africa / RCMRD. (2021). *Hydrological Modelling Framework for Operational Streamflow Monitoring in Tanzania.*  
Led development; framework underpins the live system at [streamflowmonitor.rcmrd.org](http://streamflowmonitor.rcmrd.org/).

SERVIR Eastern & Southern Africa / RCMRD. (2020). *Rapid Flood Damage Assessment — Western Kenya 2020.*  
Sentinel-1 SAR and Google Earth Engine analysis informing humanitarian response for 15,000+ affected households.
