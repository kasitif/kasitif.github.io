## Journal Articles

**Isundwa, F. K.**, et al. (2025). *Optimizing Change Detection Methods for Flood Mapping Using Polarimetric SAR*. **IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing**.  
[https://doi.org/10.1109/JSTARS.2025.3628450](https://doi.org/10.1109/JSTARS.2025.3628450)  
Systematic optimisation of polarimetric SAR change detection approaches for flood extent mapping, benchmarking decomposition-based and coherence-based methods across different land cover conditions.

---

**Isundwa, F. K.**, Marino, A., Hunter, P., et al. (2024). *Mapping and Monitoring of Water Hyacinth in Lake Victoria Using Polarimetric Radar Data*. **IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing**.  
[https://doi.org/10.1109/jstars.2024.3476938](https://doi.org/10.1109/jstars.2024.3476938)  
First application of quad-polarimetric SAR decomposition for invasive aquatic vegetation monitoring in Lake Victoria, East Africa; demonstrated seasonal infestation dynamics across the lake basin.

---

**Isundwa, F. K.**, et al. (2023). *Streamflow and Flood Prediction in Rwanda Using Machine Learning and Remote Sensing in Support of Rural First-Mile Transport Connectivity*. **Frontiers in Climate**.  
[https://doi.org/10.3389/fclim.2023.1158186](https://doi.org/10.3389/fclim.2023.1158186)  
Applied machine learning and satellite remote sensing for operational streamflow and flood forecasting to support rural infrastructure resilience in Rwanda.

---

**Isundwa, F. K.**, et al. (2019). *The Potential for Water Stewardship Partnership in Kenya*. **Arabian Journal of Geosciences**.  
[https://doi.org/10.1007/s12517-019-4506-x](https://doi.org/10.1007/s12517-019-4506-x)  
Assessed opportunities for multi-stakeholder water stewardship frameworks in Kenya, combining hydrological data and policy analysis.

## Conference Proceedings

**Isundwa, F. K.**, Marino, A., et al. (2024). *Mapping Floods Using SAR Polarimetry in Imola, Italy*. In **IGARSS 2024 — IEEE International Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium**, pp. 10799–10802.  
[https://doi.org/10.1109/igarss53475.2024.10640708](https://doi.org/10.1109/igarss53475.2024.10640708)  
Demonstrated polarimetric SAR-based flood extent mapping in an urban floodplain environment using the 2023 Imola, Italy flood event as a case study.

---

**Isundwa, F. K.**, Marino, A., et al. (2024). *Monitoring Temporal Trends to Assess Infestation of Water Hyacinth in Lake Victoria Using PolSAR*. In **EUSAR 2024 — 15th European Conference on Synthetic Aperture Radar**, Munich, 23–26 April 2024. VDE.  
[https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10659438](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/10659438)  
Presented time-series polarimetric analysis tracking seasonal water hyacinth dynamics across Lake Victoria.

---

**Isundwa, F. K.**, Marino, A., et al. (2023). *Using PolSAR to Assess Temporal Trends of Water Hyacinth Infestation in Lake Victoria*. In **2023 IEEE India Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium (InGARSS)**, Bangalore, India, 10–13 December 2023.  
[https://doi.org/10.1109/ingarss59135.2023.10490369](https://doi.org/10.1109/ingarss59135.2023.10490369)  
Initial findings from multi-temporal polarimetric classification of aquatic vegetation infestation patterns.
